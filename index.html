Galaxy is a scientific workflow, data integration, and data and analysis persistence and publishing platform that aims to make computational biology accessible to research scientists that do not have computer programming or systems administration experience

Are you new to Galaxy ? Do you need help ?
Galaxy is not suited for long-term data storage. We encourage users to promptly download and store their data securely, as any dataset older than 6 months will be subject to regular deletion. Your understanding and cooperation in ensuring the optimal performance of Galaxy for the entire community are greatly appreciated.
